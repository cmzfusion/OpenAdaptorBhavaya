The Demo Application

The Demo Application illustrates how Bhavaya could be used. It provides a real-time GUI view of data in a self-contained active database.

  • When launched the demo application should show trades as they happen in the "Trades - All" tab.
  • Switching to the "Positions - All" tab (by clicking its name) will display the effects of the trades (that are appearing in the "Trades - All" tab) on, for example, the price of a given traded instrument.
  • The cells can be seen flashing when their content changes.
  • The "Collections", "Configurations" and "Edit" labels on the left and right edges of the screen can be clicked to bring up new windows. These are Bhavaya's Tabbed Split Panels component.

Two of the application's capabilities, that may be useful to users of the software, are highlighted below.

 Display position for all trades in Euros

  • Open the Collections menu and double click on the positions entry that appears. This will bring up another window, where you can enter a name for this grouping e.g. Euros.
  • Click the plus button and select currency, then highlight EUR, click the right arrow and click OK.
  • Double click on the Euros entry that should have appeared under the positions tree in the collections tab.

You should now have a new table called "Positions - Euros", which only shows trades in Euros. The information displayed in this table can be configured using the "Edit" split panel, which has check-boxes for the information available for positions (these are actually the properties of the synthesised positions class).

 Display the total value of instruments grouped by maturity date and categorised by currency

Configure the application to only display the relevant properties:

  • Click on the "Positions - all" tab. Then open up the edit split panel, by clicking on the edit label on the right.
  • Expand the Instrument and Bond Future tree, and remove the First Delivery Date tick.
  • Also remove Quantity and Timestamp, and from Instrument/Currency remove: Description, Instrument Type and Price. Expand the Currency tree and click on code.

Pivot the data by currency, by clicking on the toggle pivoting button (menu bar, table with a highlighted blue top row and green left row).

Switch on grouping, using the menu bar item with two red arrows and then display the amount in each currency using the Edit panel Quantity tree and checking amount.

Switch the date series on by clicking on the Sigma symbol in the menu bar.

Experiment with the effect of the other buttons ...

The positions tab can be undocked from the main window, by right-clicking its name and selecting Undock Tab as Window.
